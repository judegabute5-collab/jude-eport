My School Events

A showcase of the events, activities, and experiences that have shaped my academic journey and personal growth.

MATH MONTH

During Math Month, I participated in exciting activities with my classmates. I joined the Damath competition and finished in 4th place. Even though I didn't win, the experience taught me to think fast and stay focused during competitive moments. I also participated in the Math Mural activity where our section worked together to create a meaningful and creative mural for the inter-section competition, showcasing our teamwork and artistic effort.

ENGLISH MONTH

English Month was memorable because our section worked hard together on meaningful projects. We won the door book cover competition by designing our classroom door creatively with everyone contributing ideas and effort. I also supported our class representative in the Booklandia competition, serving as a backdrop during the performance. Through poster making and slogan making activities, I learned that supporting each other, trying our best, and celebrating as a team matter just as much as winning individual awards.

MVP EVENT

The MVP event felt meaningful for our entire section as everyone showed strong support and unity for our representative. Together, we cheered for them and worked collaboratively to create an elaborate sash, which ultimately won the Best Sash award. Our representative also won a title, making our whole section proud of their achievement. This event beautifully demonstrated the core values of our section—teamwork, support, loyalty, and unity. It reminded me that success feels more special and rewarding when everyone works together and celebrates as one family.

VPOP SHOWCASE

VPOP (Variety Performance & Overall Performance) was a vibrant showcase of our school's diverse talents and boundless creativity. The event celebrated different performances, artistic expressions, and individual talents from our school community. It provided an amazing opportunity to see our fellow students shine on stage and appreciate the incredible variety of talents that exist within our school. From musical performances to dance routines to theatrical presentations, VPOP reminded us of the importance of celebrating each other's unique abilities.
